About Golborne Capital

Golborne Capital is an innovative provider of secured private credit, offering asset-based lending solution to growth companies and small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) across continental Europe and the UK. We specialize in being the first institutional lender to our portfolio companies, a key distinction that allows us to operate in a niche market with limited competition.

Our investment strategy is centred on capital preservation. We primarily make senior loans, which are secured by the borrower's financial assets. This senior position in the capital structure ensures a strong claim on assets, providing exceptional downside protection and generating a compelling risk/reward profile for our investors.

Golborne Capital is uniquely positioned to benefit from the expanding European specialty finance market and the increasing demand for sophisticated, non-traditional financing solutions. Our focus on jurisdictions with robust legal frameworks further enhances the security and resilience of our strategy, making us a trusted partner for both borrowers seeking flexible capital and investors looking for secure, high-yield opportunities.
